Biography

Xiaoyi Qu is currently a third year PhD student in the Department of Industrial and Systems Engineering of Lehigh University, working with Prof. Daniel P. Robinson. His current research focuses on constrained structured optimization problems. Recently, he has become interested in enhancing the training and inference efficiency of deep neural networks.
